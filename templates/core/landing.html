About This Project

This state-of-the-art file management platform was independently developed from scratch by Ayan Ahmed Khan, encompassing complete system architecture, backend development, frontend design, and database management. Developed under the expert mentorship of Prof. Atul Chouhan, the platform leverages cutting-edge technologies including Django, Next.js, and AI integration to deliver a robust, scalable solution tailored specifically for the MITS community's institutional requirements.
