Privacy Policy

MITS Cloud values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

  • System usage logs for security and optimization.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to operate and improve the platform, verify users, and ensure compliance with institutional policies.

  • Provide secure file storage and sharing features
  • Improve user experience and platform reliability
  • Ensure compliance with college data policies

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties except when required by law or institutional authorities.

Policy Updates
